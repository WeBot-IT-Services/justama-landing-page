About Our Company

Your Trusted Import & Export Partner in Malaysia

Established in 2021, Jutasama Import and Export Sdn Bhd has rapidly grown to become a trusted partner for Malaysian businesses seeking to expand their international reach. Based in Mont Kiara, Kuala Lumpur, we specialize in simplifying complex trade processes and providing end-to-end import/export solutions across Southeast Asia and beyond.

Our team combines deep local market knowledge with international trade expertise to deliver personalized service that larger corporations often can't match. We understand the unique challenges faced by Malaysian SMEs and have built our services specifically to address these needs with cost-effective, reliable solutions.
